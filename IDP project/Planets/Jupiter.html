The Giant of the Solar System
Biggest Planet - Jupiter is the largest planet, over 1,300 Earths could fit inside it!
Gas Giant - Made mostly of hydrogen and helium, with no solid surface.
Great Red Spot - A massive storm that has raged for over 350 years and is bigger than Earth.

Has Rings - Jupiter has three faint rings made of dust, discovered in 1979.
Many Moons - At least 95 moons, including:
Io - Most volcanic moon in the solar system.
Europa - May have an underground ocean, possibly supporting life.
Ganymede - Largest moon, even bigger than Mercury.
Callisto - Covered in craters, with potential underground water.
Fast Spinner - Spins once in 10 hours, creating super strong winds.
